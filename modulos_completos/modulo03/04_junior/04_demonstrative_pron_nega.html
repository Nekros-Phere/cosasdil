Demonstrative pronouns

Negative form

To make the negative form we useIS NOT with the singular demonstrative pronouns (THIS/THAT) and ARE NOT with the plural demonstrative pronouns.

(Para armar la forma negativa usamos IS NOT con los pronombres demostrativos singulares y ARE NOT con los pronombres demostrativos plurales.)

Examples

THIS IS NOT a flash drive.

THESE ARE NOT touchscreens.

THAT IS NOT a mouse.

THOSE ARE NOT speakers.
