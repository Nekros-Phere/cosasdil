Affirmative form

Forma afirmativa

Demonstrative pronouns are words that point to specific things.

Los pronombres demostrativos son palabras que usamos para señalar cosas específicas.

It is important to know that pronouns can appear in the singular or the plural form. Near or far.

Es importante saber que pueden aparecer de forma singular o plural. Cerca o lejos

Near (cerca) Far (lejos)
Singular This - Este/a/o That - Ese/o/a, aquél, aquella/o
Plural these - Estos/as Those - Esas/os, Aquellas/os

Examples

This is a book. (Este es un libro). 

That is a book. (Aquél es un libro).

These are books. (Estos son libros).

Those are books. (Aquellos son libros).

Did you know? - Sabías que...

We often use IS with singular demonstrative pronouns.

Con frecuencia usamos IS con pronombres demostrativos en singular

THIS IS - Esto/a es

THAT IS - Aquello/aquel es

And use ARE with plural demonstrative pronouns.

Y usamos ARE con pronombres demostrativos en plural

THESE ARE - Estos/as son

THOSE ARE - Aquellos/aquellas son
