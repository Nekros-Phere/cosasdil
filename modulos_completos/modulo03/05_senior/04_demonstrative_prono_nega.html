Demonstrative pronouns

Negative form - Forma Negativa

To make a negative form we use IS NOT with the singular demonstrative pronouns (THIS/THAT) and ARE NOT with the plural demonstrative pronouns.

Para armar la forma negativa usamos IS NOT con los pronombres demostrativos singulares y ARE NOT con los pronombres demostrativos plurales.

EXAMPLES
THIS IS NOT a flash drive. ESTO NO ES una memoria flash.
THESE ARE NOT touchscreens. ESTAS NO SON pantallas táctiles.
THAT IS NOT a mouse. ESO NO ES un ratón.
THOSE ARE NOT speakers. AQUELLOS NO SON parlantes.
